PEOPLE BROWSER
Enter the identifier of a user in the box at the top of the page, and click the Look button. A list of the people they follow appears. Then you can double-click on any name to reveal who they follow and so on. This was an experiment, and I don't think it turned out that great because most people follow a lot of people for a list like this. But maybe new ideas will spring from this. 5/29/23 by DW
